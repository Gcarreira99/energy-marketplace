# SMART CONTRACT MODELS

## CONTRACT 1 - EnergyToken.sol

Purpose: represent energy units.

Example: 1 token = 1kWh

Use ERC20

Features:

- mint energy
- transfer energy
- burn energy

## Contract 2 — Marketplace.sol

Purpose: handle trading.

Functions:

- createSellOrder()
- buyEnergy()
- cancelOrder()

Store:

- seller
- quantity
- price
- timestamp
